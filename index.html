How It Works

Seven Yoga is an online yoga school where you can learn from trusted teachers, anywhere in the world. The process is simple:

1. Choose a Teacher

Browse teacher profiles, specialties, and reviews to select the right fit for your practice.

2. Pick a Time

Choose a time slot that works for you from the teacher’s availability, all conveniently online.

3. Pay Securely

Make a secure payment through Stripe. After payment, you’ll automatically receive a Zoom link for your session.

4. Join Your Session

Connect via Zoom with your chosen teacher and begin your yoga journey from anywhere in the world.

More Than the Physical

Yoga is often seen as movement, flexibility, or fitness. At Seven Yoga, we honor the roots: it is breath, energy, awareness, balance, healing, inner wisdom. Our approach is gentle, modern, and holistic — inviting you to experience yoga as a way of living, not just a practice on the mat.

The Seven Chakras

In yoga, chakras are described as centres of energy running through the body. They can be seen as a way of understanding how our physical, emotional, and mental states are linked.


Each centre reflects qualities we all recognise — from feeling grounded to expressing ourselves clearly. Through yoga and breath, these areas are gently brought into balance.


At Seven Yoga, we use the chakra system as a simple guide. It offers insight into how body and mind connect, making practice more meaningful in daily life.
